About Us.

Yeenx Diner is a vibrant Nigerian restaurant in the heart of Lagos, offering authentic flavors with a modern twist. Since 2015, it has been a go-to spot for dishes like suya-spiced grills, signature jollof rice, crispy plantain chips, and rich efo riro. Yeenx Diner blends tradition and innovation to create a truly memorable dining experience.
